About

Stephanie Riera is a web developer based in Austin, Texas. She works for The Frontside a software consultancy that specializes in UI and building modern applications in Ember.js. Prior to becoming a developer, she received her Bachelor's in Molecular Biology from UT in San Antonio.

Stephanie is passionate about womens's empowerment and giving back to the community. She helped organize and coach the first Rails Girls

When she's not working, Stephanie enjoys biking, hiking, and spending time outside. Collecting vinyl, music festivals, and traveling are her favorite pastimes.
